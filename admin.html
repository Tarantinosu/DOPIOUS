DOPIOUS+ — Creative Design & Production Agency Thailand

DOPIOUS+ is a full-service creative design and production agency based in Thailand, delivering innovative solutions across Industrial Design, Interior Design, Exhibition & Event Design, Branding, Visual Merchandising, 3D Visualization, and Production Services.

Industrial Design

Our Industrial Design team crafts functional and aesthetic product solutions for consumer goods, retail fixtures, and custom manufacturing.

Interior Design

DOPIOUS+ delivers award-caliber Interior Design for retail spaces, corporate offices, showrooms, and hospitality environments.

Exhibition & Event Design

We design immersive exhibition stands, trade show booths, pop-up activations, and live event experiences across Thailand and Southeast Asia.

Branding

From brand strategy and identity systems to packaging and communication design, our Branding service builds cohesive, memorable visual languages.

Visual Merchandising

DOPIOUS+ provides Visual Merchandising design and consultation for retail brands to maximise in-store engagement and sales conversion.

3D Visualization

Our 3D Visualization studio produces photorealistic renders, walkthroughs, and virtual staging for architecture, interior, and product projects.

Production Service

We offer end-to-end Production Services including material sourcing, fabrication, installation, and project management.
